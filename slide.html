Some recipes will be more involved than others, but most of the time, the recipe will be a nice bite-sized chunk of information that only covers 3-5 pages or so. There are over 50 recipes in this book. I have compiled them over the last eight years from people who have asked questions on the wxPython mailing list, StackOverflow or emailed me directly.
